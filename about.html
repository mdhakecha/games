About Us

CUROSIL LIFFECARE PVT LTD works with private hospitals, wholesalers and medical distributors across India and global having mission to address and provide basic healthcare need by augmenting patents well-being offering more efficient and cost-effective work process. We are eyeing forward to grow in years to come and serve our customers with our wide range of quality healthcare products and related services. With renowned expertise in wound management, operating theatre and personal health care products. Curosil Lifecare Pvt ltd having ISO 9001:2015 and CE certification for said product.
