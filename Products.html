CURO CREPE (COTTON CREPE BANDAGE B.P.)

It consists of characteristics fabric of plain weave in which warp threads are two fold cotton threads, crepe twisted and weft threads are of cotton/viscose/both mixed. The warp threads are arranged two threads S twist and two threads Z twist repeated. It is one continuous length without joints and has fast edges.

Cotton Crepe Bandage is used in the treatment of sprains, and strains and in other conditions in which light support is required. It may be used for this purpose in conjunction with other surgical appliances. It is also used for correctional purpose.

Size: 6.00 cm X 4 mts, 8.00 cm X 4 mts, 10 cm X 4 mts and 15 cm X 4 mts
