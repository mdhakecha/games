CUROGEL-OXI FIBRIL/ LAYER

CUROGEL-OXI LAYER Absorbable Hemostat is made of a fibrous form of ORC.

  • The seven layers of CUROGEL-OXI LAYER can be peeled off and torn into strips, layers, tufts, rolls, or pads; and this makes it the perfect fit for many different wound management needs.
  • In fact, CUROGEL-OXI LAYER will adhere perfectly to the wound site, even in difficult-to-reach places and against gravity.
  • CUROGEL-OXI LAYER can conform even more effectively than CUROGEL-OXI Standard.
  • This makes CUROGEL-OXI LAYER perfect for irregular surfaces (such as tumor beds.
  • Areas particularly affected by gravitational forces (such as those beneath parenchymatous organs).
  • For hard- to-reach areas (such as those around capillary anastomoses, sinus cavities or the epidural space).
  • CUROGEL-OXI LAYER is also suitable for use with electrocautery, and when CUROGEL-OXI LAYER is used in layers it will immediately absorb any bleeding, so providing the surgeon with enhanced visibility of the wound site.
  • CUROGEL-OXI LAYER ‘melts’ into bleeding tissue, but it can still be easily removed when required.
  • The surgeon can also avoid product wastage by peeling off and using as much or as little CUROGEL-OXI LAYER as is needed.


CUROGEL-OXI KNIT

CUROGEL-OXI Knitting Absorbable Hemostat is knit woven in a higher density and stronger weave than CUROGEL-OXI Standard.

This gives CUROGEL-OXI Knitting enhanced hemostatic capabilities, making CUROGEL-OXI Knitting into an even faster acting hemostat that can achieve hemostasis 29% faster than CUROGEL-OXI Standard.

  • CUROGEL-OXI Knitting is also three times as thick as CUROGEL-OXI Standard, which gives CUROGEL-OXI Knitting a greater strength and coverage for heavier bleeding.
  • This greater strength makes CUROGEL-OXI Knitting the ideal hemostat to use in trauma, laparoscopic surgery, sternotomies, solid organ surgery, and in any procedure where the site requires wrapping.
  • CUROGEL-OXI Knitting can be also sutured in place to provide additional suture support.
  • CUROGEL-OXI Knitting is also an excellent choice of hemostat for trauma wounds, and can even be rolled up and inserted into a narrow injury such as a puncture wound.
  • CUROGEL-OXI Knitting can hold a suture and its higher-density material also maintains its structural integrity during surgery.
  • CUROGEL-OXI Knitting is preferred by many surgeons for laparoscopic procedures and, if a minimally invasive operation requires the use of an absorbable hemostasis product
  • CUROGEL-OXI Knitting can be introduced through a trocar.

CUROGEL-OXI SNOW / PUFFY

CUROGEL-OXI PUFFY Absorbable Hemostat is made of a structured non-woven fabric, needle punched with interlocking fibers.

  • This provides ease-of-use in both open and minimally invasive procedures.
  • Without sticking to instruments. CUROGEL-OXI PUFFY also has a microfibrillar structure, which gives it a greater surface area.
  • This means that CUROGEL-OXI PUFFY delivers improved speed, handling, overall performance and improved time-to-hemostasis.
  • CUROGEL-OXI PUFFY increases surface contact with the bleeding site. It also has an ability to reposition.
